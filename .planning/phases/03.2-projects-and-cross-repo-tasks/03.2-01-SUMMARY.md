## Decisions Made
- **OQ1 (terminal id scheme):** structured `{task_id}:{repo_name}:tN` + a `repo_name` field on `TerminalRecord` so per-repo RAM grouping and "close a repository" (D-10) are field lookups.
- **OQ2 (partial creation):** best-effort — `Task.repos` may hold fewer than the project's members while a creation is in flight; no auto-rollback (abort handling lands in Plan 02 `window.py`).
- **OQ3 (`WorktreeSession`):** fully replaced by `Task`; a 1-repo project is a `Task` with one `RepoCheckout`. No two parallel models.
- **caps.py untouched:** the `state.value == "active"` contract is satisfied by `Task`; verified a 3-repo Task counts as 1 active (D-14).
