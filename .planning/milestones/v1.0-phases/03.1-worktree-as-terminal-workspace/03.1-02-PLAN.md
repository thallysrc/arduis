<objective>
Pivot `window.py` from "one global LayoutModel whose leaves are worktrees" to
"one LayoutModel PER worktree whose leaves are terminals". The canvas shows the
terminals of ONE worktree; selecting a sidebar row swaps the entire workspace
(tmux: panes = terminals, windows = worktrees). A new worktree is born with 2
terminals side by side (left agent with `claude` fed, right plain shell); the
pinned `main` row becomes a regular 1-terminal workspace with no special-casing.

This is the STRUCTURAL half of the pivot. Plan 03 re-targets keybindings, RAM
summing, hibernate/resume, and close-teardown on top of this structure.

Purpose: Deliver the approved 3-level interface model (levels 2-3) from
`docs/MOTIVATION.md` — D-02, D-03, D-04, D-07, and the workspace-swap semantics
re-targeting PAR-01/PAR-02/LAYOUT-01.
Output: Refactored `src/arduis/window.py` (structure + create + swap + reflect).
</objective>
